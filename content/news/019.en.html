28.02.2017 BASTION ST-ANTOINE GENEVA, 5th prize

NYX architectes GmbH wins the 5th prize at the open architecture competition for the enhancement of the archeological site and the public square Bastion St-Antoine in Geneva. The project was selected out of 91 submissions.
