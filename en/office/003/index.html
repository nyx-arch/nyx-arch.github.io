For the realisation of the project The Circle at Zurich airport, NYX architectes is looking for an architect (80%-100%) with great creative skills and team spirit who can work in a structured way. Three or more years of experience in execution and detail planning are a prerequisite, along with good knowledge of Archicad (2D and 3D, BIM) and basics in Adobe Creative Suite and MS Office. The working language is German, further skills in French and English are an advantage.

If you feel that you match this profile and are motivated to work in a dynamic and young setting, please send your application consisting of motivation letter, cv and portfolio (PDF) to: mail@nyx-arch.ch (max. 10MB)
